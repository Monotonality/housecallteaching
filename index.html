Policies & Guidelines

Important information about scheduling and cancellations

Cancellation Policy

If a lesson needs to be canceled, please notify Adam Torres or mark the cancellation on the scheduling sheet at least 24 hours in advance. Canceled lessons without notice may not be rescheduled.

Rescheduling

Rescheduling is available at no cost with at least 1 day advance notice. This flexibility ensures you can maintain your learning progress even with schedule changes.

Materials Included

All necessary materials for your lessons are included in the cost. No additional fees for supplies, books, or learning materials.
